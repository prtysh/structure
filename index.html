A Is For Another: A Dictionary of AI

CREDITS
A is for Another response to the question: ‘what is AI’? This is an online dictionary for re-thinking intelligence - human and non-human, natural and artificial; how they are connected, and why we use it. A project by Maya Indira Ganesh (goes to website link); built by Pratyush Raman and Design Beku, Bangalore (goes to website link); text edited by Richa Kaul Padte (website link). With contributions from participants at the School of Disobedience (website link): Andrea Goetzke, Steph Holltrieu, Georgia Lummert, and Paul Wiersbinski.
