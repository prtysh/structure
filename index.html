A Is For Another: A Dictionary of AI

'A is for Another' is response to the question: ‘what is AI’? This is an online dictionary for re-thinking intelligence - human and non-human, natural and artificial; how they are connected, and why we use it.

A is for Another is a project by Maya Indira Ganesh based on a seminar at the School of Disobedience, Berlin, and built with the support of Hivos. This website does not collect your data.
